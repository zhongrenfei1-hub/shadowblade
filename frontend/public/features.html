How it works

Six stages. Four minutes. One MP4.

ShadowBlade parallelises every step of the video factory — scripting, storyboard, voice, b-roll, composition, render — and runs each one on its own GPU. The producer hands in a brief, the cluster hands back a cut.

01SCRIPT

From brief to on-brand script in under 15 seconds.

The script model is fine-tuned on your brand kit — voice, do/avoid, cap-on-adjectives, sentence length on-screen. It scores its own output before you ever see it.

  • Tone scoring 0–1 against the brand kit. Cuts below 0.7 are auto-rewritten.
  • Constraint-aware — duration, sentence cap, CTA all enforced before drafting.
  • Source-cited when you upload notes or PRDs — you can see which line each claim came from.
02STORYBOARD

Boards drawn against your brand visual system.

Each scene gets a stylised board — editorial, dolly, UI motion — that drives every downstream choice from shot selection to transitions.

  • Three style families available out of the box, infinite custom on Enterprise.
  • One board per scene reviewed in the Studio before render — change anything, re-render that scene only.
03VOICE · TTS

Cloned, brand-voiced, mastered to -14 LUFS.

Choose a stock voice or clone your own. Loudness target, breath pacing, room tone all match the platform you're shipping to.

  • 4 takes per scene — pipeline picks the most on-brand, you can audition the rest.
  • EBU R128 mastered per channel; ducks under music with one knob.
04COMPOSE & RENDER

Composition runs in parallel across the cluster.

Each scene composites, captions, colour-grades, then commits to MP4. Rush jobs jump the queue. ETAs are honest because the workers report back live.

  • L40S 48G workers with warm hand-off on spot-reclaim.
  • H.264 high, H.265, ProRes 422 HQ, 4K 60 fps.

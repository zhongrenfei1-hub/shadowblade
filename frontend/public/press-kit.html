Brand guideline

One word. "ShadowBlade." Capital S, capital B, no space, no hyphen.

Logo. Don't stretch, recolour, rotate, or add effects. Don't reproduce smaller than 16 px tall. Always allow padding equal to the height of the "S" on every side.

Colour. Accent #22D3B7 is reserved for ShadowBlade. Don't pair it with a non-brand teal.

Description. Use this one-liner: "ShadowBlade is the enterprise AI video cloud that turns a brief into a broadcast-ready cut in under five minutes."

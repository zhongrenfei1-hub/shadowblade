Most-asked

How long does the first render take?
Median first cut lands in 4 minutes 48 seconds against your brand kit. Rush priority cuts that to about 3 minutes when the cluster has a warm worker available.
Can I bring my own voice?
Yes. Upload a 30-second clean sample under Brand kits → Voice and we'll clone it. Cloned voices are usable on Scale + Enterprise plans. The clone never leaves your tenant.
Where is my render data stored?
Whichever region you picked when you created the workspace — currently eu-central-1, us-east-1, or ap-south-1. Renders never cross-region without explicit consent. Enterprise customers can self-host or BYOC.
How are reviewers added if they don't have a workspace seat?
Share a signed link from the cut. Reviewers can watch, comment, and approve from the link itself — no account required. Links expire when you decide, watermark every frame, and log every action.
What happens when I publish a new brand kit version?
All future renders use the new kit immediately. Existing cuts are flagged for re-evaluation in the Analytics drift panel — auto-fix is one click.

About ShadowBlade

We're rebuilding the video factory for the team that ships every week.

ShadowBlade started in 2024 when our founders watched their marketing team spend more time briefing a video than shooting one. Today the pipeline runs in 14 countries, against 1,200 brand kits.

Most "AI video" tools think video is a thing you generate. We think video is a thing your team produces — under brand, under deadline, on a schedule that has nothing to do with how long the render takes. That distinction shows up in every decision we make.

It shows up in the brand kit being a first-class object, not a stylesheet. It shows up in reviewers having the same workflow as producers. It shows up in the audit log retaining every decision for seven years. It shows up in the pipeline never asking your team to learn a new tool — it asks the tool to learn your team's voice.

What we believe

Brand is a constraint, not a coat of paint. Every cut renders against the kit. Drift is flagged before it ships.

Throughput beats spectacle. A team that ships ten cuts on Tuesday will out-learn the team that shipped one cinematic cut on Friday.

Auditability is a feature. Procurement, legal, and security are not blockers; they are users.

Voice & tone
Confident, plainspoken, never breathless. Lead with the customer outcome. Cap sentences at 14 words on-screen.

Do

  • Lead with the customer outcome.
  • Use single-syllable verbs.
  • Cap sentences at 14 words on-screen.
  • End with one clear call to action.

Avoid

  • Buzzwords (synergy, ecosystem, leverage).
  • Stock metaphors (journey, ladder, north star).
  • Exclamation marks anywhere on-screen.
  • More than two adjectives in a row.

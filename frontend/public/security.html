Data protection

  • AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, automatic key rotation every 90 days.
  • Customer-managed encryption keys on Enterprise (BYOK · AWS KMS · GCP).
  • Per-workspace data isolation; no cross-tenant model training.
  • Renders deleted from origin within 30 days unless retained by policy.

Identity & access

  • SAML SSO with Okta, Microsoft Entra, Google Workspace, JumpCloud.
  • SCIM 2.0 provisioning — hourly sync or webhook-driven.
  • Role-based access; custom roles on Enterprise.
  • Required MFA, session timeouts, IP allow-lists, hardware-key support.

Region & sovereignty

  • Data residency pinning to eu-central-1, us-east-1, ap-south-1.
  • Renders never cross-region without explicit consent.
  • Self-host or BYOC on Enterprise — same workspace, your hardware.
  • Schrems II-compliant transfer mechanisms for EU customers.

Auditability

  • Tamper-evident audit log, retained 7 years on Scale / Enterprise.
  • Streaming export to Splunk · Datadog · Snowflake · S3.
  • Every render, comment, approval, brand-kit change is logged.
  • Signed share-links with watermark + download tracking.

Incident response

  • 24×7 oncall · 15-min SEV-1 acknowledgement.
  • Customer-facing post-mortem within 5 business days.
  • Notification within 72 h of any confirmed personal-data breach.
  • Quarterly tabletop with named CSM on Enterprise.

Vendor management

  • Every subprocessor listed, with country and purpose.
  • 30-day public notice on any subprocessor change.
  • Annual penetration test by an external firm — report on request.
  • Bug-bounty program live since 2024-09.

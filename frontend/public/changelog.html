Changelog

What shipped, and why.

Every visible change in ShadowBlade — features, fixes, performance, brand updates. New entries every Wednesday. Subscribe via RSS or email.

2026-05-21

Studio · multi-track timeline

FeatureStudio
  • Track-based timeline for video, voice, captions, and music. Snap, ripple, and zoom controls.
  • Playhead scrubbing now reflects compose progress in real time.
  • Captions auto-promote from voiceover with per-word timing — editable in place.
2026-05-14

Brand kits · drift detection

BrandAnalytics
  • Every render is checked against the active kit on completion. Drift is surfaced in Analytics and the Inbox.
  • One-click auto-fix re-renders the affected cut with the corrected tokens.
  • Brand v3 schema adds voice profile, tone do / avoid, and cap on adjectives as enforced constraints.
2026-05-07

Render cluster · L40S rollout

PerformancePipeline
  • Default worker pool upgraded to NVIDIA L40S 48 GB. Median compose time down 31%.
  • Region ap-south-1 (Mumbai) opened — pin via Settings · Region.
  • Spot-instance reclaim now triggers a warm hand-off instead of cold restart.
2026-04-30

SSO · Microsoft Entra + Google Workspace

FeatureSecurity
  • One-click connect for Entra and Google Workspace. Existing Okta tenants continue unchanged.
  • SCIM provisioning is now hourly by default; manual sync on demand.
  • IP allow-list moves out of preview — configurable per workspace.
2026-04-23

Templates · 12 net-new

FeatureTemplates
  • Twelve new templates including Monthly recap · 90s, Press quote · 20s, and Onboarding loop · 45s.
  • Templates now pre-tune to your brand kit before the first preview.
  • "Save current as template" lifts any project into a reusable surface in one click.
2026-04-16

API · webhooks for the full pipeline

FeatureAPI
  • New events: render.queued, render.started, render.completed, review.requested, review.approved.
  • Signed payloads, 30-day delivery retries, replay from the dashboard.
  • Sample receivers in TypeScript, Python, and Go in the docs.
2026-04-09

Reviewer experience · in-frame comments

FixStudio
  • Comments now cite a precise word range or timeline position, not just the scene.
  • Mention-to-assign works from any comment.
  • Review state on the Dashboard updates without refresh.
